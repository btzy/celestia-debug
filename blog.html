Check out the official celestia.io subreddit here!

There has also been some changes to celestia.io. Red stars fired by your opponents can now collide with yours and turn into food, which might be helpful when you're being fired at. In addition, eating food now makes you slower for a brief moment, so you might want to be on a lookout while eating away!

11 February 2017

Welcome to celestia.io! celestia.io is a game where you eat to live and live to eat at the same time (basically whats commonly known as an .io game). Eating food makes you bigger, and when others die they become your food. All of this happens in a galaxy far, far away…

Click here to play the game. Feel free to share any feedback and suggestions you may have on reddit or in the comments below.
